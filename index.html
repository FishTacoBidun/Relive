About the Map

Relive is a Minecraft adventure map based on the game Observation Duty. It was created 100% vanilla, no mods or datapacks, just redstone and command blocks. The goal of the game is to traverse through different floors using an elevator and escape from floor 100. Each floor has a chance to contain 1 of 60 different anomalies, some subtle, some deadly. The first time you enter floor 1, it won't have an anomaly, so you know what to look for going forward. Every 10 floors, you will receive a checkpoint. Incorrectly guessing whether there’s an anomaly or dying will send you back to your most recent checkpoint. Can you escape floor 100?
